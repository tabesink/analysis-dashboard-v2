# P15-02 Header-Detected Damage Channels

## Summary

Refactored Inspect Damage channel extraction from a fixed first-21 positional slice to deterministic header detection:

- Detect numeric source columns whose cleaned title contains `P_UG_` and `Force` or `Momt`.
- Preserve source-order internal keys as `Ch01`...`ChNN`.
- Store/display cleaned CSV/RSP titles as damage channel labels.
- Reuse the same detector for retained-artifact backfill and pending channel-map reprocessing.
