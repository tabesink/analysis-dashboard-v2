## Summary

Durability schedule upload/save now drive client-side `damage_calculation` progress polling, prerequisite report display, and a schedule repair loop when validation fails.

## Implementation

- `apply-schedule-damage-response` resolves `damage_task_id` vs `damage_prerequisite_report` from schedule save/upload responses.
- `damage-calculation-store` mirrors channel reprocess: scoped polling, close-only modal, inline banner, active-task reuse.
- `DerivedDataOperationModal` and `DerivedDataProgressPanel` support `damage_calculation` phases and failure summary action to reopen the schedule editor.
- `DurabilitySchedulePanel` shows compact `DamageValidationReportSummary` above the table and highlights affected editable fields inline.
- Saving corrected schedule rows clears prior reports and automatically starts a fresh damage task when prerequisites are current.
