## Tests

- `tests/server/services/test_derived_data_lineage.py` — lineage recording, plot-only LTTB kind, Pending stale marking, Approved immutability, reprocess regeneration.

## Stale policy

- **Pending events:** marked `stale` when `channel_map_snapshot_id` differs from the active snapshot for the program/version.
- **Approved/Obsolete events:** remain `current`; historical snapshot references are not rewritten.
- **Reprocess:** `save_channel_map_and_process_artifacts` regenerates rows and sets status back to `current`.
